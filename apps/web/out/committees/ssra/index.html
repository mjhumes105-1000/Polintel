Senate Rules and Administration Committee

The Senate's operational committee — governing chamber procedures, elections, campaign finance, and congressional administration.

SENATE RULESELECTIONSCAMPAIGN FINANCECAPITOLVOTINGPROCEDURES

WHY THIS MATTERS

Senate procedures — including filibuster reform, holds, and unanimous consent — fall under this committee's purview. Election security legislation, voter registration modernization, and campaign finance disclosures all require its action.

WHAT IT DOES

The Senate Rules Committee governs Senate procedures, election administration, campaign finance law, the Library of Congress, Government Publishing Office, and Capitol facilities. It handles federal election legislation, including voting rights and campaign finance reform bills that reach the Senate floor.
