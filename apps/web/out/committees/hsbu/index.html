House Budget Committee

The committee that sets the overall framework for federal spending and revenue — the blueprint every other committee must follow.

BUDGETDEFICITRECONCILIATIONFISCAL POLICYSPENDINGCBO

WHY THIS MATTERS

Reconciliation — the process that passed the Affordable Care Act, the 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, and the Inflation Reduction Act — originates here. The budget resolution also sets the parameters within which the Appropriations Committee must operate.

WHAT IT DOES

The Budget Committee writes the concurrent budget resolution that sets overall spending limits, revenue levels, and deficit targets for the federal government. It also handles reconciliation instructions that allow major legislation to pass the Senate with a simple majority.
