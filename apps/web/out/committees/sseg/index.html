Senate Energy and Natural Resources Committee

Energy production, federal public lands, and national parks — the Senate committee managing America's natural resources and energy future.

ENERGYPUBLIC LANDSNUCLEARRENEWABLESNATIONAL PARKSOIL AND GAS

WHY THIS MATTERS

Energy transition legislation, offshore drilling authorizations, national park management, and federal land use decisions all flow through this committee. Its actions on energy permitting directly affect electricity prices and carbon emissions trajectories.

WHAT IT DOES

The committee oversees federal energy policy including oil and gas leasing, nuclear power, renewable energy programs, and electricity markets. It also manages public lands, national parks, forests, and the federal mineral leasing program through the Interior Department.
