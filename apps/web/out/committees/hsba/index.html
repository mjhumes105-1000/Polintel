House Financial Services Committee

Banks, housing, crypto, insurance, the Fed — if money moves through a regulated institution, this committee sets the rules.

BANKINGFINANCEHOUSINGCRYPTOFEDWALL STREETCONSUMER PROTECTION

WHY THIS MATTERS

Mortgage availability, student loan servicing, credit card fees, bank capital requirements — these policy decisions determine whether ordinary Americans can access affordable credit and whether Wall Street faces meaningful guardrails. The committee's deregulatory or regulatory posture ripples through the entire economy.

WHAT IT DOES

Financial Services oversees all federal financial regulation: banking supervision, securities markets, housing finance, insurance, and cryptocurrency. It exercises oversight of the Federal Reserve, Treasury, SEC, FDIC, and CFPB.
