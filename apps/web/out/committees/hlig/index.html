House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence

The only committee with full visibility into what the CIA, NSA, and sixteen other intelligence agencies are actually doing.

INTELLIGENCENATIONAL SECURITYCIANSASURVEILLANCECOVERT ACTION

WHY THIS MATTERS

Surveillance programs, covert action overseas, signals intelligence — all of it falls under this committee's watch. When intelligence failures happen (or are prevented), this committee is the accountability mechanism. Its classified annex to the annual Intelligence Authorization Act sets spy agency budgets and missions.

WHAT IT DOES

HPSCI oversees the entire U.S. Intelligence Community — its budgets, authorities, collection programs, and covert operations. Members receive classified briefings unavailable to the rest of Congress and can compel the intelligence community to answer questions it refuses everyone else.
