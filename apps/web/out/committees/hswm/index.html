House Ways & Means Committee

Every tax bill, Social Security change, and Medicare policy starts here — the oldest and most powerful committee in Congress.

TAXESTRADESOCIAL SECURITYMEDICAREHEALTHCARE

WHY THIS MATTERS

Tax rates, trade policy, the future of Social Security, drug pricing through Medicare — the committee's decisions touch every American's paycheck and retirement. Members routinely shape trillion-dollar policy with amendments that receive almost no media attention.

WHAT IT DOES

Ways & Means has exclusive jurisdiction over all federal tax legislation, trade agreements, Social Security, Medicare, and Medicaid. The Constitution requires revenue bills to originate in the House, and by tradition, they originate in Ways & Means.
