House Energy & Commerce Committee

The broadest jurisdiction in the House — energy, healthcare, telecom, the internet, and public health all land here.

ENERGYHEALTHCARETELECOMBROADBANDFDAENVIRONMENTCONSUMER PROTECTION

WHY THIS MATTERS

Drug pricing, broadband access, hospital mergers, electric vehicle policy, natural gas exports — this committee's footprint touches nearly every household in America. Its healthcare subcommittee alone oversees spending that rivals the entire Pentagon budget.

WHAT IT DOES

Energy & Commerce holds the widest legislative portfolio of any House committee: it covers healthcare (FDA, CDC, NIH policy), energy production, environmental regulation, telecommunications, broadband, and consumer protection. If it plugs in or flows through a pipe, E&C has a say.
