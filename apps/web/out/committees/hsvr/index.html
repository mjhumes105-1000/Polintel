WHY THIS MATTERS

With over 18 million veterans and roughly 9 million enrolled in VA healthcare, the committee's decisions directly affect the quality of medical care, benefits timeliness, and support for transitioning service members. The PACT Act, which expanded benefits for toxic exposure veterans, passed through this committee.

WHAT IT DOES

The Veterans' Affairs Committee oversees the Department of Veterans Affairs, the largest integrated healthcare system in the country. It authorizes VA healthcare programs, disability compensation, education benefits (GI Bill), home loans, and burial benefits for America's veterans.
