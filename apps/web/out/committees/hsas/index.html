House Armed Services Committee

A $900 billion defense budget, every military base, and the rules of engagement for U.S. forces worldwide — Armed Services controls it all.

DEFENSEMILITARYNDAAPENTAGONWEAPONSNATIONAL SECURITY

WHY THIS MATTERS

Defense contracts, base employment, weapons procurement, and military personnel policy flow from the NDAA. The committee also shapes U.S. military strategy and authorizes the use of armed force. Members from defense-heavy districts use it to protect local bases and attract contracts.

WHAT IT DOES

HASC writes the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) — the must-pass annual legislation that sets military pay, weapons programs, base closures, and force structure for every branch of the armed forces.
