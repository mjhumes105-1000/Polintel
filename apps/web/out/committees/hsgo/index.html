House Oversight and Government Reform Committee

The House's primary investigative engine — subpoena power over the executive branch, federal agencies, and beyond.

OVERSIGHTINVESTIGATIONSGOVERNMENT REFORMEXECUTIVE BRANCHACCOUNTABILITY

WHY THIS MATTERS

When executive branch misconduct is alleged — from agency scandals to White House conduct — this committee has the authority to compel testimony and documents. Its investigations can shape public opinion, drive prosecutions, and force policy changes without passing a single bill.

WHAT IT DOES

The Oversight Committee investigates the efficiency and integrity of the federal government, issues subpoenas to agencies and officials, holds hearings on executive branch conduct, and advances government reform legislation. It covers federal workforce policy, postal service, and D.C. governance.
