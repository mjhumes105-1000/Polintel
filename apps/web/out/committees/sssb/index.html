WHY THIS MATTERS

Small businesses represent the largest source of new job creation in the economy. SBA pandemic relief programs, loan guarantees, and federal contracting set-asides are vital lifelines for Main Street businesses. The committee works to reduce regulatory burden and expand credit access for entrepreneurs.

WHAT IT DOES

The Senate Small Business Committee oversees the Small Business Administration and writes legislation supporting America's 33 million small businesses. It covers SBA loan programs, federal procurement preferences, disaster assistance, and entrepreneurship development programs.
