House Transportation & Infrastructure Committee

Highways, bridges, airports, public transit, Amtrak, waterways, and FAA safety — infrastructure that Americans use every day starts here.

INFRASTRUCTUREHIGHWAYSAVIATIONTRANSITRAILPORTSFAA

WHY THIS MATTERS

The Bipartisan Infrastructure Law sent $1.2 trillion through projects this committee shaped. Reauthorization of highway programs, airport modernization, rail safety after disasters like East Palestine, and public transit funding for major cities — all require this committee's action.

WHAT IT DOES

The committee writes surface transportation bills, aviation authorization legislation, water resources development acts, and oversees the FAA, FTA, FRA, Coast Guard, and federal flood insurance. It also manages federal infrastructure grants and formula funding.
