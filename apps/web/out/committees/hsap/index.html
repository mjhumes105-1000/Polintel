House Appropriations Committee

This committee writes the federal checkbook — every dollar the government spends must flow through here.

SPENDINGBUDGETDEFENSEDOMESTIC PROGRAMSEARMARKS

WHY THIS MATTERS

When Congress decides how much money the Pentagon gets, whether the EPA can hire inspectors, or how big NIH research grants are — Appropriations makes that call. Members routinely direct funding to their districts in ways that reshape local economies and employment.

WHAT IT DOES

The Appropriations Committee drafts and passes the twelve annual spending bills that fund every federal agency and program. Nothing in the federal discretionary budget moves without its approval.

JURISDICTION

All discretionary federal spending (roughly $1.7T annually)Annual defense and non-defense appropriations billsEmergency supplemental spending packagesGovernment shutdown negotiationsEarmarks and congressionally directed spendingContinuing resolutions and stop-gap funding
