Senate Judiciary Committee

Federal judges, the Supreme Court, immigration law, and DOJ oversight — the Senate committee that shapes American law for generations.

WHY THIS MATTERS

Every Supreme Court justice, every circuit court judge, and every district court judge serves only after this committee signs off. Its hearings on judicial nominees and DOJ conduct are among the most consequential proceedings in government — the outcomes shape constitutional interpretation for decades.

WHAT IT DOES

Senate Judiciary reviews and confirms all federal judges — from district courts to the Supreme Court. It also has jurisdiction over immigration, antitrust, criminal justice, intellectual property, and oversight of the Department of Justice and FBI.
